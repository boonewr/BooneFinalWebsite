MILO

  • Bio
    Milo is a fluffy orange and white cat. Despite his long fur I've never seen him even slightly dirty or disheveled-looking.
  • Personality
    Milo is the most nocturnal cat I know. Most of the cats around here sleep seemingly-randomly, but Milo I rarely ever see during the day. This level of night activity is not convenient for taking picutres.
