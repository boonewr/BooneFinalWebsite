BISCUIT

  • Bio
    Biscuit is a tan and white cat with a permanent mean look on her face.
  • Personality
    Despite her grim expression biscuit is a very sweet cat. There used to be a planter box on the porch where she lives, that is until Biscuit drove the plants extinctby sleeping on them. Even post-flora she slept in it so much one of the sides fell off.
