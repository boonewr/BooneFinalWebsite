BISCUIT

  • Bio
    Biscuit is a tan and white cat with a permanent mean look on her face.
  • Personality
    Despite her grim expression biscuit is a very sweet cat. There used to be a planter box on the porch where she lives, that is until Biscuit drove extinct by sleeping on top of the plants. Even after the plants were dead she slept in it so much one of the sides fell off.
