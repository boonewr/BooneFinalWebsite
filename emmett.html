EMMETT

  • Bio
    Emmett is a large tabby cat with big eyes. He has one (biological) sister - Lucy.
  • Personality
    Emmett is very friendly to just about anyone. He has the unique character trait of attemtping to throw himself in your path when you try and walk away. He is fast for his size - I often have to sprint to escape his clutches.
