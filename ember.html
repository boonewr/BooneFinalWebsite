EMBER

  • Bio
    Ember is a small tortoiseshell cat. She has mostly black fur with small flecks of orange - hence the name ember. Ember is my cat and she has one brother - Sid.
  • Personality
    Ember is extremely shy towards strangers and other cats. She is an accomplished huntress and enjoys being outside in the rain.
